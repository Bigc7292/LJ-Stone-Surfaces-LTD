## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### What is the difference between Marble and Quartz?
Marble is a natural stone, each slab is unique but can be more porous and sensitive to acidic substances. Quartz is an engineered stone, highly durable, non-porous, and requires less maintenance.

### Do you offer installation?
Yes, we provide professional installation services for all our stone products.

### How do I maintain my stone surfaces?
For natural stone like marble, we recommend sealing regularly and using pH-neutral cleaners. Quartz surfaces are low-maintenance and generally only require soap and water.

### Can I see the slabs in person?
Yes, visitors are welcome at our New York showroom. We recommend calling ahead for a personalized tour of our current inventory.
